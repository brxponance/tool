## Using Idea Screening Page 16590

Launch it with FactSet Search: @ISC

Idea Screening provides a workflow-driven, customizable application that allows you to enter your screening criteria to generate new buyer lists and identify potential investments. It allows you to screen and report on Companies, [M&A](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/15444), Private Equity/Venture Capital, Public Equity Offerings, and Private Placements.

You can choose to view FactSet's customizable preexisting screens or create your own screens based on criteria you specify. Then, use your screen results to target potential investments, customers, investors, and buyers or to identify precedent transactions.

To get started, choose from any of the topics in the left pane.
