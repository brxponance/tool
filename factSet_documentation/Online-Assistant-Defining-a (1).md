## Getting Started

To get started, click the **Holders** button at the top of the [Universal Screening (@US)](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/20595) home page.

You can choose from the following options in the Suggested Screens section:

-   Click the **New Holder Screen** ![](online-assistant/27251.html) button to create a blank report.
-   Click on the **Starter Screen** button, to get some basic result columns on the Holders universe.
-   Click a predefined Holder Screen (e.g., Hedge Funds by Country) to open a report that has a FactSet-established universe and format. Each demonstrates the main features of the application and can serve as building blocks for creating your own screens.

[Top of Page](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/21801#top)

## Managing Universe Limitations

Use the Select Criteria pane to specify your ownership or equity universe. If you are creating a new screen, the Select Criteria pane is expanded by default. The universe is already selected if you select a predefined screen, but you can adjust that as needed.

![](online-assistant/27251.1.html)

Select your universe by applying any of the fields in category tabs. The category options and definitions are as follows:

**Ownership**: Use this tab to limit holders by ownership type (e.g., stakeholder investor type, holding style and equity assets). 

**Equity**: Choose this tab to limit by equity universe (e.g., country, sector and industry). 

**Identifier List**: This tab allows you to limit your screen by a set of securities stored in your saved OFDB file/s. 

[Top of Page](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/21801#top)

## Adding and Removing Limits

You can add or remove specific limits within your screen. 

-   Enter or select an item to include it in your universe and add it to the top of the Select Criteria pane. Press the CTRL key to select multiple criteria from the same category.
-   You can also modify your criteria by removing individual securities. To remove an item from your current limitations, click the adjacent **Remove** button. Additionally, you can click the **Clear All** button in the top right corner to remove all of the holder screening criteria.

![](online-assistant/27251.2.html)

## Results Pane

The Results pane allows you to evaluate the outcome of your screen's universe and data formatting. You can also add additional columns, groupings or filters to your results.
