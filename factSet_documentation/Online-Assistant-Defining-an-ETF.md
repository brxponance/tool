## Getting Started

To get started, click the **ETF** button located at the top of the application [Home](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/20595#home) page.

You can choose from the following options in the Suggested Screens section: 

-   Click the **New ETF Screen** ![](online-assistant/26018.1.html) button to create a blank report. All U.S.-listed ETFs are included in the default universe. 
-   Click the **Starter Screen** button to get some basic result columns on the default U.S.-listed ETF universe. 
-   Click a predefined ETF screen (e.g., US ESG) to open a report that has a FactSet-established universe and format. Each demonstrates the main features of the application and can serve as a building block for creating your own screens.

## Managing Universe Limitations 

Use the Select Criteria pane to specify your universe of ETFs. If you are creating a new screen, the Select Criteria pane is expanded by default. If you selected a predefined screen, the universe is already selected, but you can adjust that as needed.   

![](online-assistant/26018.2.html)

Select your universe by applying any of the fields in the top category tabs shown. The category options and definitions for ETF screens are as follows:

-   Economic Exposure and Geographic: These tabs cover the [FactSet ETF Classification System](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/17753#ETFClassification) and are linked. Any changes made to one of these fields will impact the remaining fields.
-   Investment Strategy, Key Facts, and Fund Structure: Choose these tabs to select criteria to further restrict your universe (e.g., excluding all geared funds).
-   Scoring & Analytics: Choose this tab to inspect an ETF at a more granular level using Efficiency, Tradability, and Fit scores. The tab covers the [FactSet ETF Scoring Methodology](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/17753#ETFScoring), which is the first wide-ranging and robust methodology for evaluating, comparing, and contrasting ETFs.
    
    |**Note**|Access to the Scoring & Analytics category requires a separate subscription. Contact your FactSet Salesperson for information on how to gain access.|
    |---|---|
    
-   Identifier List: This tab allows you to limit your screen by a set of securities stored in your saved OFDB file/s. 

### Browsing for Limits 

Hover over each category pane and scroll down its list menu to see specific criteria options. Alternatively, you can click each category's adjacent **Lookup** ![](online-assistant/26018.3.html) button and start typing in the populated Search box to quickly find a criteria item. 

### Adding and Removing Limits 

Click an item to include it in your universe and add it to the top of the Select Criteria pane. Press the **CTRL** key to select more than one criteria item from a category.

You can also modify your criteria by removing individual securities. To remove an item from your current limitations, click the adjacent **Remove** ![](online-assistant/26018.4.html) button. Additionally, you can click the **Clear All** button in the top right corner to remove all of the ETF screening criteria.
