## Building Your Screen

### Screening criteria

The private placements universe covers public companies that are listed on a U.S. exchange and have disclosed the terms of the placement with the SEC. Idea Screening allows you to screen on and display issuer information, deal terms, investors, agents, and legal counsel involved in the transaction.
