## Managing Universe Limitations

Use the Select Criteria pane to specify your corporate governance, campaign, or proxy proposal universe. If you are creating a new screen, the Select Criteria pane will be expanded by default. If you selected a predefined screen, the universe is already selected, but you can adjust that as needed.

Select your universe by applying any of the fields in the top category tabs shown. The category options and definitions for corporate governance and campaign screens are as follows. 

### Corporate Governance 

![](online-assistant/26198.html)

-   <u>Company</u>: Use this tab to specify the characteristics of companies covered by SharkRepellent (e.g., including all listed on the S&P 500).  
-   <u>Governance</u>: Choose this tab to select criteria pertaining to bullet proof ratings, charter/bylaw defense changes, and more. 
-   <u>Poison Pill</u>: Choose this tab to inspect corporate governance at a more granular level using current poison pill statuses, adoption dates, and more. 
-   Identifier List: This tab allows you to limit your screen by a similar universe of securities that are stored in your saved OFDB file(s) (e.g., watch lists and portfolios). 

### Campaign 

![](online-assistant/26198.1.html) 

-   Campaign Summary: This tab allows you to limit your universe based on general campaign characteristics such as status, objective, and announcement dates.  
-   Campaign Details: Select this tab to dictate whether activism campaigns against funds are a part of your universe, and to specify the type of activist tactics and demands included.  
-   Equity: Choose this tab to include only campaigns that are affiliated with specific indices, FactSet sectors/industries, market values, and more. 
-   Activist: This tab lets you narrow your campaign universe based on specific activist criteria such as holder type and threat level. 
-   Proxy: Use this tab to refine your universe based on specific proxy fight winners, meeting dates, and more. You can also refine it based on what Institutional Shareholder Services Inc. (ISS) and Glass Lewis support.
-   Identifier List: This tab allows you to limit your screen by a similar universe of securities that are stored in your saved OFDB file(s) (e.g., watch lists and portfolios).

### Proxy 

![](online-assistant/26198.2.html)  

-   Proposal: Use this tab to limit your universe based on basic proposal characteristics such as result, category, and meeting dates. 
-   Equity: Select this tab to include only proposals that are affiliated with specific indices, FactSet sectors/industries, market values, and more. 
-   Identifier List: This tab allows you to limit your screen by a similar universe of securities that are stored in your saved OFDB file(s) (e.g., watch lists and portfolios).

### Browsing for Limits

Click each category pane and scroll down its list menu to see specific criteria options. There are also form fields and fields with drop-down menus. Use the up and down arrows or type in a number to customize each metric.   

![](online-assistant/26198.3.html)

### Adding and Removing Limits

Enter or select an item to include it in your universe and add it to the top of the Select Criteria pane. When available, select a category's "All" option to include every item listed in it. 

Modify your criteria by removing individual limitations. To remove a current limitation, click the adjacent **Remove** ![](online-assistant/26198.4.html) button. Additionally, you can click the **Clear All** button in the top-right corner to remove all of the screening criteria. Use the Exclusions drop-down menu to [omit](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/20606#Understanding) inactive securities, secondary listings, or non-equity securities from your universe.
