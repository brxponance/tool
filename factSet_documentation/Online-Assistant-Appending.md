## Appending Securities Page 20924

Launch it with FactSet Search: @US

Use the Append tab to add additional securities to your Equity, ETF, or Debt screening report. Compare and contrast the results of your appended securities with those that meet your screening criteria, or continue analyzing securities that were unable to pass the investment criteria you specified.

This feature makes it easy to discover how securities in your report are performing relative to a select basket of peers. All data items in your report are included for the appended securities – regardless of whether or not they pass your universe or parameter limitations. 

Within the Append tab, click the **Lookup** button ![](online-assistant/26152.html) or use type-ahead searching to search for a security identifier. Once you are finished with your selections, a number will appear at the top of the tab indicating how many securities have been added: 

![](online-assistant/26152.1.html) 

A Pass ![](online-assistant/26152.2.html) or Fail ![](online-assistant/26152.3.html) icon will populate next to each appended security to indicate whether or not it meets your screening criteria. You can place your cursor over the icons to see the parameter limitations that the security did and did not pass. 

For example, this screen is limited to the S&P 100 Index. Additionally, due to an established parameter, the screen only includes index constituents that have a Price / Earnings ratio greater than 25. Although the 3M Company is listed on the S&P 100, it has a P/E less than 25; therefore, the Fail icon is shown: 

![](online-assistant/26152.4.html) 

However, if the minimum P/E limitation is changed from 25 to 15, the Pass icon will be shown for the 3M Company: 

![](online-assistant/26152.5.html) 

Once you've finalized your security selections, click the **Download to Excel** button ![](online-assistant/26152.6.html) to export your screening results for further analysis. By default, each appended security is listed at the top of the spreadsheet, so you can easily compare their results with the securities that meet your screening criteria.

|**Note**|Up to 30 securities can be appended to new screens, however; if a saved legacy screen containing more than 30 securities is opened in the new application, all securities will be shown.
|---|---|
The appended identifiers do not affect your results or universe totals.|
