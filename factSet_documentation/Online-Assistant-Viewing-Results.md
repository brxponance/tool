## Viewing Results Page 16699

Launch it with FactSet Search: @ISC

Click the **View Results** button to analyze your results based on the criteria that you’ve selected.

Click the **View Results** button ![](online-assistant/20147.html) to view your results in the Results pane. Your report results are broken into multiple pages depending on the number of entities that passed your criteria. You can browse through your results by entering a page number or clicking the Next/Previous page buttons at the bottom of your results.

The report includes multiple levels of information to highlight the relationships between entities such as PE/VC firms, funds, and portfolio companies. Expand/collapse in-line view results to display the specific entity detail.

![](online-assistant/20147.1.html)

### Resizing the Results Pane

Hover your mouse over the pane border and click and drag or use the expand/collapse arrows ![](online-assistant/20147.2.html) to maximize/minimize the Results pane.

### Sorting Results

Hover over any column name, and select the drop-down menu to sort your report in ascending/descending order or filter based on the column you select. You can also double-click a column name to automatically sort your report by that column.

### Finding Results

You can search for a specific screening result by using the search bar above your results. If the company or transaction you're searching for is among your passing results, you will see a link directly to it:

![](online-assistant/20147.3.html)

If the company or transaction did not pass the screen, the search bar will declare that the result is not found and display a "Why?" link. You can click the link to see the parameters that the company or transaction did and did not pass.

![](online-assistant/20147.4.html)

|**Note**|The Find feature is unavailable in the Investments pivot for PE/VC screening or in the Buyers pivot in M&A.|
|---|---|

**Filtering Results**

You can filter results to only view entities within an in-line view that passed your search limitations or to show all entities, regardless of your current limitations.

|**Note**|The functionality to filter in line view results based on search limitations is available in the following content sets: Private Equity/Venture Capital, Public Equity Offerings, and Private Placements.|
|---|---|

By default, when you're expanding in line views, the results are filtered to only show the entities that pass your screen, which allows you to easily view targeted results based on your defined [search parameters](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/16697#adding). You can hover over the view's **Information** icon to see how that view is filtered. For example, you can see in the following screen that the results are filtered by the search parameter limiting the screen to portfolio companies in the Auto Parts industry:

![](online-assistant/20147.5.html)

If you prefer to view all results within an in line view, click the "X of Y Passed" label next to the appropriate heading and then select "Total" from the drop-down menu as shown in the example below:

![](online-assistant/20147.6.html)

### Creating Custom Comps Lists

Once you've run a screen, you can right-click any row and select "Add to Comps" to begin creating your own custom comps list. This allows you to assemble only the results you want into a single place for further analysis. Browse through the results to find transactions, or click the **Find/Add** button to search for any transaction, whether or not it appears in your results.

|**Note**|You can also add rows using just your keyboard by selecting any row or group of rows and pressing the **ENTER** key to add them to a custom comps list.|
|---|---|

Select multiple consecutive rows by selecting the first row, then pressing and holding the **SHIFT** key before selecting the last row. Select multiple, nonconsecutive rows by pressing and holding the **CTRL** key and selecting each row that you want. In either case, press **ENTER** to add the comps or **DEL** to remove them.

### Deleting Results

Company screens give you the option to delete a row from your results list by clicking the Delete icon in the first column, or by selecting the row and pressing the **DEL** key. Deleted companies then collect at the bottom of the window under the "Deleted" link. Click the link to open a list of all deleted companies, where you can restore them all or each one individually.

![](online-assistant/20147.7.html)

### Changing the Report View

Idea Screening lets you change your report view for a different perspective on the data. Depending on your content set, you can view:

-   Transactions Report: Arranges results based on the transaction. The Transactions Report only has top-row line items
-   Volume Total Report: Arranges transactions based on industry, location, or date
-   League Table Report: Arranges transactions based on Legal or Financial Advisor

|**Note**|You can present Volume Total and League Table results graphically by clicking the **Chart** ![](online-assistant/20147.8.html) button.|
|---|---|

### Downloading Your Results

You can also click the **Download to Excel** button ![](online-assistant/20147.9.html) and select to view all your results, or just top-level rows (where applicable) within Microsoft Excel. A summary of the screen that was used to generate your downloaded results is displayed as the last worksheet in your Excel spreadsheet. Your screen results are then displayed in the additional worksheets. For screens with multiple levels of information, such as PE/VC screens, the results are broken out by the lowest level of detail provided in your Idea Screening results.

### Jumping to other FactSet applications

You can further investigate your passing companies by jumping them to other FactSet applications. The first column in each result links to other FactSet applications, such as Company Guide or Deal Detail reports, depending on your content set. Click the link's down arrow to open that company in Company Snapshot or view the company's domicile in [Country Synopsis](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/15382) or open company-specific information such as it's Entity Structure, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow.

![](online-assistant/20147.10.html)

### Viewing Results in Microsoft Excel

To view your results in Excel, first save a screen that you created in Idea Screening. Once the screen is saved, choose the screen's Summary tab and copy the IS2\_SCREEN code plus Screen ID. You can find these under Screen Details.

![](online-assistant/20147.11.html)

In Excel, enter a security ticker followed by a caret into one cell, and then paste the copied IS2\_SCREEN code plus Screen ID into an adjacent cell.

![](online-assistant/20147.12.html)

With the codes in place, click the **Refresh** button from the FactSet ribbon in Excel.

![](online-assistant/20147.13.html)

## Editing Screen Results

Click the **Edit Report** button to customize your report's column display. If you have already run the screen and have the report open, you can also launch the report editor by right-clicking anywhere in the report and selecting "Edit Report." Either method launches the Edit Report dialog box where you can add or remove items or reorder the column displays.

Choose the tabs on the far left to access items for the report's main and in-line views. The top tab corresponds to the report's main view, followed by tabs named for each of the inline views. For example, the Investment Round tab contains items you can find in the Investment Round inline view.

![](online-assistant/20147.14.html)

The following list displays the editing options available in the Edit Report dialog box. Use Idea Screening's [hot key combinations](https://my.apps.factset.com/oa/pages/pages/17198) to adjust the column selections using your keyboard.

## Changing Your Results Display

### Reordering Report Columns

Click and drag a report column header to reorder it within each level of your results.

### Removing Report Columns

Hover over a report column header, and select "Delete Column" from the drop-down menu.

### Resizing Columns

Hover your mouse over a column border and click and drag to adjust its size.
